About AquaVitals

AquaVitals is an AI-powered tool designed to help fish hatcheries in Iowa optimize fish survival through predictive modeling. Using real environmental data — including temperature, rainfall, and water clarity — our system forecasts fish health outcomes to support smarter, data-driven decisions.

Built on a multi-stage regression pipeline, AquaVitals models how environmental stressors affect fish over time. It is modular, scalable, and grounded in real-world data from the Decorah Fish Hatchery, making it practical for hatchery managers and researchers alike.

This project was created as part of the Pi515 AI Challenge by a passionate group of student innovators focused on sustainable agriculture and environmental technology.
