About AquaVitals

AquaVitals is an AI-powered tool built to help fish hatcheries in Iowa predict fish survival outcomes using environmental data. By analyzing temperature, rainfall, and water clarity, we provide accurate, actionable insights to support decision-making at every stage of fish care.

The platform is based on a multi-stage regression pipeline and trained on real-world data from the Decorah Fish Hatchery. It’s designed to be modular, scalable, and easy for both scientists and hatchery managers to use.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable aquaculture by equipping hatchery teams with tools that improve survival rates and reduce operational uncertainty. We believe data should drive decisions — and our goal is to make predictive intelligence accessible and practical for every hatchery.

AquaVitals was developed as part of the Pi515 AI Challenge by a team of student innovators passionate about agriculture, sustainability, and environmental technology.
